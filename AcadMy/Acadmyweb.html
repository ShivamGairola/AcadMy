We at AcadMy

Test Series

Length Test

AcadMy offers Length Test, you can give Part Test where short length exam of full-length test can be given, Full Test where Full-length exam in accordance of exam pattern can be given and Topic Test where test of questions are based on a particular topic. Based on your preference choose your type.

Practice Mode

AcadMy offers Practice Mode, where you can choose from various huge set of questions. Practice mode is all about the learning process, it is not a test environment.Using this students can learn at their own pace and and place for students to become confident with the concepts of a tutorial

RB Questions

Questions based on recommendation of the user experience. The platform will work applying machine learning algorithms on previous data collected from user's performance data. This let's the student to go through the weak topics more to strengthen the concept.
